Artificial Intelligence

AI is neither magic nor a fad. Used well, it is force‑multiplication. Used poorly, it is expensive chaos and compliance risk.

Where we apply AI

  • Identifying high‑leverage AI opportunities in your products and internal workflows.
  • Designing LLM‑powered features, agents and tools with clear boundaries and fallbacks.
  • Building retrieval‑augmented generation (RAG) and semantic search on top of your data.
  • Classic machine learning (tabular / time‑series) for prediction, ranking and scoring.
  • Natural Language Processing: classification, extraction, summarization, translation, NER.
  • Computer vision pipelines where needed (document understanding, image classification, etc.).

How we deliver it safely

  • Choosing between off‑the‑shelf APIs, fine‑tuned models and bespoke deployments.
  • Data, privacy and compliance posture for AI usage (PII, PHI, trade secrets, regulations).
  • Evaluation frameworks for AI quality: golden sets, human‑in‑the‑loop review, A/B tests.
  • MLOps: experiment tracking, model registry, CI/CD for models, monitoring and rollback.
  • Monitoring quality, drift and cost so AI features stay trustworthy over time.

We help you move from “we should do something with AI” to concrete capabilities that your teams can understand, operate and evolve safely.
