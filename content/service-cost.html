Operational Expenses

Cost is a signal. Either it reflects deliberate investment, or it reveals chaos.

FinOps foundations

  • Full cloud cost review: compute, storage, networking, data transfer, licenses.
  • Tagging and account structure so cost signals are trustworthy.
  • Unit economics: cost per customer, transaction, request or feature.

Structural optimization

  • Architecture and infra changes that remove structural waste, not just “turn things off”.
  • Rightsizing, autoscaling policies, reserved instances / savings plans.
  • Storage lifecycle, data egress management and region selection.

Controls and visibility

  • Budgets, alerts and dashboards that make spend visible to the right people.
  • Guardrails and policies that prevent surprise bills and runaway experiments.
  • Partnering finance and engineering so unit economics are understood and owned.

We do not optimize for the cheapest possible system. We optimize for the system that gives you the most leverage per dollar, sustainably.
