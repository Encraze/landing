Performance & Scalability

Fast is a feature. Predictable is survival.

Finding the bottlenecks

  • End‑to‑end latency and throughput analysis — from browser or mobile app to database.
  • Profiling of hot paths: CPU, memory, I/O, database queries, external calls.
  • Slow query analysis, indexing strategies and schema tuning.

Designing for scale

  • Capacity planning, load testing and SLO design for realistic growth curves.
  • Performance‑oriented architectures: caching, queues, back‑pressure, fan‑out / fan‑in.
  • Event‑driven vs synchronous flows, batch vs streaming decisions.
  • Frontend performance: bundling, caching, CDNs and perceived‑latency improvements.

Making performance a habit

  • Observability that exposes reality: metrics, traces, logging and meaningful dashboards.
  • Performance budgets and guardrails integrated into CI/CD.
  • Turning performance work into a continuous habit, not a one‑off “war room”.

We reveal where time and resources are really spent, then carve a path to systems that stay fast when your user base and data volumes are no longer “reasonable”.
