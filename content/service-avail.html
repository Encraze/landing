Stability & High Availability

Outages are expensive, but living in permanent firefighting mode is worse.

Designing resilient systems

  • Design and review for resilience: redundancy, graceful degradation, failure domains.
  • Multi‑AZ / multi‑region strategies, data replication and failover.
  • Backup and restore design, and actually testing restores under pressure.

SRE & incident management

  • SLO / SLA definitions that reflect real customer expectations and error budgets.
  • Incident response playbooks, on‑call rotations and communication patterns.
  • Game days and chaos‑style exercises that expose weak spots before production does.
  • Post‑incident analysis that fixes systems, not just symptoms or individuals.

We stabilize the platform and the way teams respond to failure, so “keeping the lights on” stops consuming all of your creative energy.
