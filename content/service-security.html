Security & Compliance

Security is not a checklist. It is a discipline that must quietly wrap every decision you make: architecture, code, infrastructure, vendors, people.

Application & platform security

  • threat modeling for products, platforms and data flows.
  • Secure‑by‑default architecture reviews of critical components and data paths.
  • Secure SDLC: coding standards, reviews, dependency hygiene and secrets management.
  • SAST/DAST, container and dependency scanning, and integration into CI/CD.
  • Authentication, authorization and identity design: IAM, RBAC, least privilege.

Cloud & infrastructure security

  • Cloud security baselines across AWS/GCP/Azure: networks, storage, keys, logging.
  • Secrets management, key rotation and certificate lifecycle.
  • Network segmentation, zero‑trust patterns and exposure reduction.

Compliance & operational security

  • Controls, policies and evidence that satisfy ISO27001, SOC2 and related frameworks.
  • Vendor risk management and security reviews.
  • Incident runbooks, escalation paths and post‑incident learning that actually sticks.
  • Business continuity and disaster recovery design, including backup and restore drills.

We step in as the calm center between engineering, legal, risk and audit: translating requirements into design and work items, then ensuring controls live in your code, not just in documents.
