People & Leadership

Great systems are built by great teams. Great teams do not happen by accident.

Hiring and team formation

  • Org and role design: which teams you actually need, how they interact, and why.
  • Hiring frameworks and interview loops for backend, frontend, data, ML, SRE, product and leadership.
  • Scorecards and rubrics that make “what good looks like” explicit for each role.
  • Onboarding flows so new people become effective in weeks, not months.

Growth, feedback and performance

  • Competency / skill matrices per track (IC, manager, architect, TPM, etc.).
  • Growth paths and promotion criteria that are transparent and credible.
  • 1:1 structures, feedback loops, peer review and recognition that encourage learning.
  • Performance management that is fair, data‑informed and human — including PIPs when needed.
  • Companion structures for remote / hybrid teams, including communication and collaboration norms.

Leadership coaching

  • Coaching for tech leads, managers and heads of engineering stepping into bigger scope.
  • Decision‑making frameworks, delegation and ownership boundaries for leaders.
  • Conflict resolution and escalation patterns that do not poison the culture.

We help you build an organization where people know what good looks like, how to grow towards it, and how to make decisions without waiting for a hero.
